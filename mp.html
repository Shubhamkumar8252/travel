Top Places to Visit in Ujjain

Mahakaleshwar Temple

One of the 12 Jyotirlingas of Lord Shiva and the most revered temple in Ujjain.

Ram Ghat

A sacred bathing ghat on the Shipra River, vibrant during the Kumbh Mela.

Kal Bhairav Temple

Unique temple dedicated to Kal Bhairav, where liquor is offered as part of the rituals.

Sandipani Ashram

Ancient gurukul where Lord Krishna is believed to have studied under Guru Sandipani.
