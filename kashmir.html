Top Places to Visit in Vaishno Devi

Vaishno Devi Bhawan

The main shrine located at Trikuta Hills, visited by millions of devotees each year.

Ardh Kuwari Cave

A holy cave where Goddess Vaishno is believed to have meditated for 9 months.

Bhairavnath Temple

Located near the main shrine, dedicated to Bhairav, whose darshan is essential after Vaishno Devi.

Charan Paduka

A sacred rock believed to bear the footprints of Goddess Vaishno Devi.
