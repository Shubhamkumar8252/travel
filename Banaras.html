Top Places to Visit in Banaras

Kashi Vishwanath Temple

A revered Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, one of the holiest shrines in India.

Manikarnika Ghat

The primary cremation ghat in Varanasi, offering a glimpse into the cultural and spiritual practices of the city.

Banaras Hindu University

One of the oldest and largest residential universities in India, known for its sprawling campus.

Assi Ghat

One of the most peaceful ghats, popular for yoga, boat rides, and morning walks.
